Craft Hash

Two origins of hash under one roof — California ice-water solventless and traditional Moroccan heritage.

No. 01 — Concentrate

Full-spectrum resin, captured with nothing but ice, water and patience.

Our Craft Hash is made the way the best hash has always been made — by washing whole, sun-grown flower in ice water to free the resin heads, then sieving, drying and curing them into a concentrate that carries the plant's full terpene signature. No solvents, no shortcuts, no compromise on flavour.

We press it three ways: a glossy, hand-rolled temple ball for the connoisseur; loose, golden bubble hash for versatility; and a clean pressed coin for everyday luxury. Alongside the California line sits our imported traditional Moroccan hash — heritage product from the world's most storied hash origin, brought in through regulated partners.

How it's made

  • Fresh and dried sun-grown flower from Vista Ranch, Anderson Valley
  • Multi-screen ice-water wash to isolate full-melt resin heads
  • Freeze-dried and hand-cured for stability and aroma
  • Graded by micron and melt before it ever earns the Prime Origins name

On the palate

Expect a slow, resinous melt with notes that shift by cultivar — gassy and pine-forward from the California washes, warm and spiced from the Moroccan slabs. Every batch ships with its own record of where it was grown, washed and pressed.
