Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026

1. Who we are

Prime Origins Limited (“Prime Origins”, “we”, “us”) operates this website at https://primeoriginsglobal.org. We are a private limited company registered in England and Wales under company number 15775663, with registered office at 128 City Road, London, EC1V 2NX, United Kingdom. We are the data controller for personal data collected through this site. For privacy queries, contact oliver@primeorigins.org.

2. What data we collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact details — name, email, company/organisation and the message you send when you submit a partnership enquiry or contact us by email.
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type, device and pages visited — collected, only with your consent, via Google Analytics, and through standard server logs kept by our hosting provider for security.

This is a brand and information website. We do not process payments, and we do not operate a user account or marketplace here.

3. How we use it (legal bases)

We process personal data on the following bases under UK GDPR:

  • Legitimate interest — to respond to your enquiry, discuss potential partnership or investment, and to operate, secure and improve the website (including aggregate analytics).
  • Consent — for analytics cookies, and for any optional marketing communications, withdrawable at any time.
  • Legal obligation — to meet our regulatory, accounting and tax obligations.

4. Who we share data with

We share personal data only with the following categories of recipient, each bound by appropriate data-processing terms:

  • FormSubmit — delivers our partnership-enquiry form submissions to us by email.
  • Google (Google Analytics) — aggregate website analytics, loaded only after you accept cookies, with IP anonymisation enabled.
  • Professional advisers — legal, accounting and regulatory, where relevant.
  • Authorities — where legally required.

We do not sell personal data.

5. International transfers

Some of our processors (notably Google and FormSubmit) are based outside the UK/EEA. Where data is transferred internationally we rely on adequacy decisions or Standard Contractual Clauses to ensure equivalent protection.

6. How long we keep it

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out above, or as required by law. Enquiry records are deleted within 24 months of last contact unless an ongoing business relationship requires longer retention.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Have inaccurate data corrected;
  • Request erasure (subject to legal retention obligations);
  • Restrict or object to processing;
  • Receive your data in a portable format;
  • Withdraw consent (including for analytics) at any time.

Exercise any of these rights by emailing oliver@primeorigins.org. You also have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ico.org.uk).

8. Cookies

We use a small number of cookies and similar technologies. Analytics cookies are set only with your consent. See our Cookie Policy for the full list and how to change your choice.

9. Security

We use standard technical and organisational measures including HTTPS encryption and access controls. No system is perfectly secure — if you believe an incident has occurred please contact oliver@primeorigins.org.

10. Minors

This website concerns regulated cannabis products and is intended for adults, licensed trade partners and professional or qualified investors only. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version is always available at https://primeoriginsglobal.org/privacy.html with the “last updated” date shown above.
