Grown in the Anderson Valley sun.

Vista Ranch sits high in the redwood country of Mendocino County, inside the certified Navarro River Cannabis Appellation. Everything you see here was photographed on the farm — real plants, real soil, real terroir.

The place

A farm built to give back more than it takes

Sixty thousand square feet of licensed canopy, draped across a south-facing hillside ringed by redwoods. Hoop houses extend the season; smart pots and engineered living soil feed the plants; and watershed restoration keeps the Navarro River healthier each year.

This is regenerative, full-term, sun-grown cultivation — the slow way, the right way. The proof is in the resin.
