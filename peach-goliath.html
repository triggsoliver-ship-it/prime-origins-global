Peach Goliath

A giant, high-yield CBD cultivar — available as flower and as certified seed.

No. 07 — Flower & Seed

A CBD/CBG/CBC cultivar bred for size, yield and stability.

Peach Goliath lives up to its name — an extremely vigorous plant that can reach in excess of 12 feet, yielding up to 7.5 lbs per plant. It carries a rich CBD profile with supporting CBG and CBC, and stays compliant on THC when harvested correctly.

Offered as CBD flower and as certified, feminized seed — a proven choice for growers planting at field scale.

Field guide

From greenhouse to harvest

Start in greenhouse

3–4 weeks prior to your desired field-planting date.

Plant in field

Most environments late May or early June; varies by location.

Begins flowering

July through to early September (varies by location).

Flowering time

7–8 weeks — monitor THC levels through flower.

Harvest

Around 5 weeks after the first flower.

Turn time

5–6 months plant life, start to finish.

Seeds per acre

2,500 — or 3,000 if planted as late as July.

Certified seed

Certified to preserve genetic purity and varietal identity (Georgia Crop Improvement Association, an AOSCA member).
