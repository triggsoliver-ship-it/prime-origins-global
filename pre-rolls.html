No. 04 — Pre-Rolls

Real, sun-grown flower in every roll — accessible craft, done right.

A pre-roll is only as good as what's inside it. Ours are filled with milled whole flower from the same harvest as our jarred buds — never shake, never trim-filler, never the leftovers other brands hide in a paper. It's the easiest, most approachable way to experience Prime Origins craft.

Each one is packed to an even density so it draws smoothly and burns cleanly to a fine white ash — the tell-tale sign of properly grown, properly cured cannabis. Priced to be an everyday pleasure, made to a standard that isn't.

What's inside

  • Whole-flower from Navarro River sun-grown harvests
  • Milled and packed to an even, smooth-drawing density
  • Slow-cured flower for a clean, full-flavoured burn
  • Single-strain and curated blends, all documented
