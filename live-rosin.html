Live Rosin

Heat, pressure and nothing else — the purest, most aromatic expression of sun-grown flower.

No. 02 — Concentrate

Solventless, full-spectrum, and bottled at the peak of terpene expression.

Live Rosin is the pinnacle of solventless craft. We start with fresh-frozen, sun-grown flower — picked at peak ripeness and frozen immediately to lock in the living terpene profile — then ice-water wash it into premium hash and press that hash under gentle heat and pressure. What comes out is a glossy, buttery rosin that tastes exactly like the plant smelled in the field.

Because there are no solvents anywhere in the process, nothing is stripped and nothing is added. It's the cleanest, most honest concentrate we make.

From flower to jar

  • Fresh-frozen flower, washed within hours of harvest
  • Full-melt ice-water hash, freeze-dried before pressing
  • Low-temperature press to protect delicate terpenes
  • Cold-cured to a rich badder texture, then jarred
Method
Solventless · pressed
Input

On the palate

Bright, layered and unmistakably alive — citrus and florals up front, gas and cream underneath, with a smoothness that only solventless can deliver. Each jar is tied to the harvest and cultivar it came from.
