A premium hash house with two origins of supply.

01

What we are building

A premium hash house: sun-grown, appellation-certified cannabis cultivated in Anderson Valley and processed into craft hash, rosin, flower and pre-rolls — alongside an import line of traditional Moroccan hash.

02

Why now

Hash and solventless concentrates are the fastest-growing premium category in cannabis, and Morocco's regulated framework has opened a legal route for traditional hash for the first time.

03

Why we win

Our founding team built California's cannabis appellation system. Our Moroccan partners supply heritage product no domestic competitor can copy. Provenance is documented on the Prime Origins platform.

California · Cultivate

Vista Ranch, Anderson Valley

  • 60,000 sq ft licensed canopy — full-term outdoor and hoop house
  • Navarro River Cannabis Appellation — certified sun-grown terroir
  • Regenerative methods: smart pots, engineered soil, watershed restoration
  • 2,094 lbs bud + 837 lbs trim projected — graded into flower, rosin, hash and pre-rolls
Morocco · Import

Traditional Moroccan hash

  • Established partner farms operating inside Morocco's regulated framework (ANRAC)
  • Centuries-old craft from the world's most recognised hash origin
  • Documented chain of custody — COAs, batch records, export files
  • A heritage product line domestic competitors cannot replicate
